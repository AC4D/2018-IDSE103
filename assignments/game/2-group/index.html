IDSE103: Game Facilitation
Group #2: Kaley + Jay

Name of Game

First: Everyone gets a character Name your Character - write their name on the front of the card Write a 3 sentence background story about your character on the back. Who are they? What do they do? Where did they come from?

Next: Stand up! Hold your character. When you hear the music start - move about the room When the music stops - put yourself in a group of 3 with the people closest to you and find a piece of butcher paper on the wall.

Then: Introduce your character to the group 10 seconds each Write short story using all three characters. Draw a scene for the story. Ideate a story arc with a conflict and a resolution, and draw a scene that clearly represents one part of the story and involves all characters. Put your characters on the paper in the scene too. (4 min)

Last: Each team share their story. (1 min)
