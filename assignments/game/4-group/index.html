Group #4: Mariangela + Joshua

Name of Game

Instructions... Every person in a group will receive one random scene in the form of a sentence. In a group of 10, two people will receive one scene and there will be 5 scenes in total. They will have 4 min to draw that scene with as much detail as possible in order to accurately convey what the scene is Once theyâ€™re done they will share their drawings with the group member that drew the same scene They will share feedback with the other person with the same scene, and think on what they can improve for 1 minute Once feedback gets provided, each person will go back to their seat and iterate on their scene for 2 min After they are done, each person will hang all of their 4 drawings from the same scene next to each other Everyone in the team will now try to guess what the scene was trying to convey. Constraints: NO devices allowed to reference objects, people or postures Outcome: The purpose of the activity will be to practice our ability to convey a single scene on paper/whiteboard as fast as possible.
