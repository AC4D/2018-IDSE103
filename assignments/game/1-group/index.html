IDSE103: Game Facilitation
Group #1: Adam + Noah

Scene Study

Players:

  • For 5-15 participants

Materials:

  • One piece of 11x14 paper for each participant
  • Different sized black markers, at least one per participant
  • (1) stopwatch
  • Tape or pins for posting artword on the wall afterward

Instructions:

  1. Take one piece of paper.
  2. During the game, everyone participating will add one thing to each piece of paper to create a scene.
  3. Take 10 seconds to make a doodle on your piece of paper so itâ€™s not just a blank page when the game starts.
  4. Pass your paper to the person on your left when finished.
  5. You will have one minute to draw something on your paper. When finished, you will pass the paper to the person on your left.
  6. Repeat, switching the hand you use to draw for each round, until each person has added something to the piece of paper.
  7. Behold the creations!
