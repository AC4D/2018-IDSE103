IDSE103

Design and Facilitate a Game

You and a group will design an original game and facilitate it. One group will present each class, see syllabus for schedule.

You will also submit a written set of instructions so that anyone can replicate your game.

Outcomes

  • Energize a group admist a mid-day slump
  • Promote camaraderie and mutal trust
  • Be fun

Constraints

  • No longer than 15 minutes
  • Scale from 5–15 players
  • Only require materials commonly found in office (post-its, whiteboard, copier paper, etc)
  • Name your game

Grading

Criteria Points Possible
Facilitation was organized and effective 25
Players felt energized and bonded 25
Constraints were respected 25
Written instructions are clear and stand on their own 25
