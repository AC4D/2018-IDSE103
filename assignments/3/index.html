IDSE103 - Assignment #3

Interface Development

Part 1: Scanned high quality sketches of four screens in your app.

Part 2: Using the Apple UI Design Resources, create high fidelity mockups of those four screens completed in Sketch or Adobe Experience Design

Part 3: Prototype your "calculator" screen in HTML, CSS, and Javascript so that it functions.

Constraints

  • Sketches should be based on those done in class, but should be reworked at a higher attention to detail (alignment, spacing, etc).
  • High fidelity mockups should be pixel-perfect and indistingushable from production quality.
  • HTML prototype should also be high-quality and must include 3 inputs and an output.
