Physical Prototyping and Rendering

Part 1 - Blueprints

Using a vector drawing program (e.g. Adobe Illustrator or similar) draw your physical product idea from 3 views: top, side, and front.

Export drawings as SVG and insert into submission page.

Part 2 - Prototype

Build full-size prototype using cardboard, foamcore, or other rapid model-making material.

Photograph prototype in 2 distinct setups:

  • A - Studio setting with even lighting, no people, and seamless background
  • B - The object in it's appropriate contextal environment, actively being used by a person.
Retouch both photos to eliminate prototype shortcomings and enhance clarity of idea.

Insert both raw and retouched photos into submission page at a minimum of 3840 Ã— 2160 pixels.

Shoot both photos horizontal.

Bring model to class.
