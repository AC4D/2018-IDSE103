IDSE103 - Assignment #2

Physical Prototyping and Rendering

Part 1

Using a vector drawing program (e.g. Adobe Illustrator or similar) render physical product idea from 3 views: top, side, and front.
Export drawings as SVG and insert into submission page.

Part 2

Build full-size model using cardboard, foamcore, or other prototyping material.
Photograph model in a studio-like setting (even lighting, no people, seamless background).
Photograph model in it's appropriate environment (e.g. grocery store) and stage it being actively used by a person. (Include hands and humans for scale.)

Retouch both photos to eliminate model shortcomings and enhance clarity of idea. Insert both raw and retouched photos into submission page.

All photos should be submitted at no less than 3840 Ã— 2160 pixels.

Bring model to class.
