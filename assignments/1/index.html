IDSE103 - Assignment #1

Time-Based Storytelling

Beginning from app sketches done in class, complete 4 distinct storyboards demonstrating the use of each of your app's features.

Choose 1 storyboard and produce a video of it.

Video should have a protagonist, make the viewer emapthize with their problem, and clearly illustrate the value of the solution.

Constraints

  • Storyboards should be medium-fidelity with at least 2 line weights.
  • Video should be less than 2 minutes long.
  • Video should should give enough context to stand on it's own and not require additional foreward or explanation.
  • Video can be shot and edited entirely on your phone.

Inspiration and Examples

  • A Taste of Home
  • Microsoft Courier
  • Apple Watch Commercial 'Find'
  • Pasta 'N More
  • Geriatric Robots
  • DropBox MVP
  • Next Stop Train App
  • Understanding Comics - Book

Tools and Tutorials

  • iMovie - iOS
  • Adobe Premier Clip - iOS + Android
  • KineMaster - Android
  • Apple Clips - iOS

Other Logistics

  • Storyboards should be scanned at no less than 5120 pixels wide.
  • Retain all raw media files in original, high-resolution formats for later revisions.
  • Videos should be uploaded to Youtube.
  • Follow assignment submission instructions.
