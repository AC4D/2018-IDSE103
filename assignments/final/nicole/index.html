How should women feel when engaging with intimates?

To start the ideation process, I thought back to my high school days of working in lingerie retail, identifying the breakdowns and missed opportunities that exist in the intimates world.

Because this is such a personal experience for every woman, helping her feel positive emotions throughout the process is essential. So, in creating this suite of tools, I identified the following three emotions to design around.

First thing’s First. Fit is Fundamental.

Did you know that more than half of the women in the US wear the wrong bra size? That’s a whole lot of missed comfort and confidence opportunities.

Before going any further, make sure you’re rockin' the right size with Flawless Fit: the pioneer feature from Grand Reveal.



Grab your bras and look for:

  • gaps or spillover in the cups
  • loose bands and straps
  • underwire poking out

About the Author

Hi! I am a student at the Austin Center for Design, where I am studying interaction design and social entrepreneurship. Prior to embarking on this education, I worked in market research, designing surveys for private equity firms and their portfolio companies. I love the contrast in research approach here: taking a richer, deeper dive into each person vs. abstracting them to a number. I look forward to continuing this challenging and rewarding work, and to seeing where my intrigues guide me to next.
