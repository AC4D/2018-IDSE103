Feedback Pod
Toolkit for Freelance Creatives.

Communication is vital for human interaction, and so it has become more and more indispensable as technology advancements make it easier for us to stay connected. The series of products that are part of Feedback Pod, aim to improve the communication between creatives - out of ideas? have a question? Feedback bud - a network of creatives and followers can guide you back to the path of creation. What about clients? Nowadays, the number of freelancers is increasing at an unimaginable rate - but what if you still need clients F2F that can give you the green light before you proceed with your work? That is what Feedback Pod is for, a home device that will let you reach out to your client with a tap of a button. What if you are a product designer, and you and your clients can't wait to see how it looks in its intended setting? Sparing some rendering time can save both designers and clients time and resources, if designed elements come pre-loaded to our AR application. Clients will just need to point, select, move and take a screenshot of how their product will look.
